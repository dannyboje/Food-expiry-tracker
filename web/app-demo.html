FRESH AHEAD

Your Smart Kitchen Companion

Track food, reduce waste, and always know what's in your fridge.

01 — PANTRY

See everything at a glance

All your items with colour-coded expiry status — red, orange, and green — so you know exactly what needs attention.

02 — SCAN

Scan a barcode in seconds

Point your camera at any product. Fresh Ahead pulls the name, brand, and health score instantly.

03 — ALERTS

Get notified before it's too late

Smart notifications remind you in time to actually use the food — not the morning after it's gone off.

04 — SHOPPING

Your list updates itself

Expired items are automatically added to your shopping list. Tick them off as you shop.

05 — HEALTH

Make healthier choices

Every product gets a health score based on Nutri-Score, NOVA group, and nutritional data.
