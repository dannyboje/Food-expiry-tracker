🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 May 2026  ·  Fresh Ahead by Yoganjali Consultancy Services Ltd

  • We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data
  • All your data stays on your device — always
  • No account or sign-up required
  • No analytics, no tracking, no ads
  • Barcode scans send only the barcode number to food databases
  • Recall data is fetched from public government APIs and matched locally

1. Who we are

Fresh Ahead is developed by Yoganjali Consultancy Services Ltd. For privacy questions contact: contact_us@yoganjaliconsultancy.co.uk

2. Data stored on your device

The App stores all data locally on your device only. We do not operate any servers that receive your data. This includes: pantry items, household profile, photos, shopping list, consumption history, and notification preferences.

3. External network calls

Barcode lookups — when you scan a barcode, only the barcode number is sent to Open Food Facts (openfoodfacts.org) and USDA FoodData Central to retrieve product information. No personal data is transmitted.

Food recall checks — the App fetches public recall lists from the US FDA, USDA FSIS, and UK Food Standards Agency. Your pantry contents are matched against these lists entirely on your device. Nothing from your pantry is ever sent externally.

4. Permissions

  • Camera — to scan barcodes and photograph expiry dates
  • Photo Library — to select existing photos of expiry dates
  • Notifications — to send local reminders before items expire (no push server involved)

5. On-device ML

The App uses Google ML Kit for text recognition to read expiry dates from photos. All processing happens on your device — no photos are sent to external servers.

6. Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Your rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control at all times. You can delete individual items within the App or remove all data by uninstalling the App.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
