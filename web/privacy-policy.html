Fresh Ahead is a food expiry tracker app developed by Yoganjali Consultancy Services Ltd. This Privacy Policy explains how the Fresh Ahead mobile application ("the App") handles information when you use it.

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at: contact_us@yoganjaliconsultancy.co.uk

3. External API calls

The App makes limited network requests to look up product information and food safety recalls. No personal data is included in any of these requests.

Barcode lookups

  • Open Food Facts (openfoodfacts.org) — an open-source, non-profit food database. Only the scanned barcode number is sent. No user data is transmitted.
  • USDA FoodData Central (fdc.nal.usda.gov) — a US government food nutrition database. Only the barcode number is sent. No user data is transmitted.

These lookups only occur when you actively scan a barcode. The barcode number itself is a product identifier with no link to you or your device.

Food safety recall checks

The App fetches public food safety recall lists from government agencies to alert you if a product in your pantry has been recalled. The following public APIs are used:

  • US FDA — api.fda.gov/food/enforcement.json
  • US USDA FSIS — www.fsis.usda.gov/fsis/api/recall
  • UK Food Standards Agency — data.food.gov.uk/food-alerts

Recall data is downloaded and matched against your pantry items entirely on your device. Your pantry contents are never sent to any external server.

5. On-device machine learning

The App uses Google ML Kit for on-device text recognition to read expiry dates from photos. This processing happens entirely on your device — no photos or text are sent to Google or any other server for processing.

6. Children's privacy

The App is designed for general audiences and is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

7. Data security

All data is stored in your device's local application storage, which is protected by your device's operating system security. We do not have access to this data. If you delete the App, all locally stored data is permanently removed.

8. Data retention

Data is retained on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App. The App automatically removes pantry items 20 days after their expiry date and keeps a rolling history of the last 500 consumption events and 20 recent scans.

9. Your rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You can:

  • View all stored data directly within the App
  • Delete individual items at any time
  • Clear all data by deleting and reinstalling the App

There is no account to delete or external data to request because we hold no data about you.

10. Third-party services

The App is built on the Expo framework and React Native. Expo may collect limited diagnostic and crash data to improve the platform. Please refer to Expo's Privacy Policy for details. This is separate from any data the App itself handles.
